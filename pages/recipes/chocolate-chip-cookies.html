Chocolate Chip Cookies

This decadent, homemade treat is perfect for spending a cozy day indoors. Grab your hot chocolate (coming soon) and get ready to make one of my favorite desserts! Each cookie has a beautiful crust, and will melt in your mouth. Top it off with a warm and gooey chocolate chips on top, and you will burst after trying these entrancing sweets. Any time of the year is perfect for chocolate chip cookies!

Now these are your classic, homemade chocolate chip cookies. With lot's of trial and error, I have finally developed a method to make a treat that bakes into a crisp and delicious dessert. I guarentee that these cookies will be satisfying!

The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies In Town

Here are some of the reasons why you would want to make these rolls:

  • Easy to make
  • Foods made at home taste better
  • Single rise
  • Creamy and gooey
  • Perfect for any occasion

Key Ingredients

  • Billspury Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

Steps to Success

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Unwrap the dough and break apart
  3. Place the pieces of cookie dough on a baking tray
  4. Put tray in oven, bake for 12-15 minutes
  5. Take out and add frosting
  6. Done!
