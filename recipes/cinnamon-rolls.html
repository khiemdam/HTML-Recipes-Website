Cinnamon Rolls

This decadent, homemade treat is perfect for spending a cozy day indoors. Grab your hot chocolate (coming soon) and get ready to make one of my favorite desserts! Each roll has a beautiful cinnamon swirl, and will melt in your mouth. Top it off with a warm and sticky cream on top, and you will burst after trying these entrancing sweets. Any time of the year is perfect for cinnamon rolls!

Now these are your classic, homemade cinnamon rolls. With lot's of trial and error, I have finally developed a method to make a treat that rises evenly. I guarentee that these rolls will be satisfying!

The Best Cinnamon Rolls In Town

Here are some of the reasons why you would want to make these rolls:

  • Easy to make
  • Foods made at home taste better
  • Single rise
  • Creamy and gooey
  • Perfect for any occasion

Key Ingredients

  • Billspury Jumbo Cinammon Rolls
  • Billspury Cream Cheese Frosting

Steps to Success

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Unwrap the dough by twisting the Billspury container
  3. Place the 5 rolls on a baking tray
  4. Put tray in oven, bake for 12-15 minutes
  5. Take out and add frosting
  6. Done!
